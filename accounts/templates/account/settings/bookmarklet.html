build Troubleshooting

The bookmarklet doesn't work on some sites expand_more
Some websites have strict security policies (CSP) that block external scripts. Unfortunately, the bookmarklet won't work on these sites. Try right-clicking on images and copying the image URL directly.
I can't drag the button to my bookmarks bar expand_more
Try right-clicking on the button and selecting "Add to Bookmarks" or "Bookmark This Link". You can also manually create a bookmark and paste the bookmarklet code.
No images are found on the page expand_more
The bookmarklet looks for images with common formats (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP) that are at least 100x100 pixels. Very small images, icons, and SVGs are filtered out to show only bookmarkable content.
